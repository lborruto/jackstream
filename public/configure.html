jackstream

Configure your Jackett + TMDB credentials. They are encoded into the addon URL and never stored on this server.

LAN IPs are auto-converted to an HTTPS URL via local-ip.medicmobile.org so Stremio clients on other devices can connect without cert warnings.
Fill the form to generate your addon URL.
HTTPS is required outside 127.0.0.1. See the README for reverse-proxy examples.
